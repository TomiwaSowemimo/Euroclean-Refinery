About Euroclean

Africa's most ambitious clean refinery project

Euroclean Refinery and Petrochemical Nigeria Limited is developing a greenfield crude oil refinery on 864 hectares in Warri, Delta State, Nigeria.

Nigeria produces 2.5 million barrels of crude oil daily, yet imports most of the refined products it needs, creating a major opportunity for local value addition.

  • Zero sulfur output
  • Zero gas flaring
  • Natural gas powered

Refining Process

Crude in. Clean fuels out.

01

Separation

Crude oil is heated and separated into fractions by boiling point in distillation columns.

02

Conversion

Heavier fractions are broken down into lighter more valuable products using heat pressure and catalysts.

03

Treatment

Every product stream is desulfurised to achieve near-zero sulfur content then blended to specification.

FAQ

Common questions answered.

Where is the Euroclean refinery located?

Our greenfield site spans 864 hectares on the outskirts of Warri Delta State in the heart of the Niger Delta Nigeria's primary oil producing region.

What stage is the project currently in?

Euroclean Refinery is currently in the design and engineering phase actively engaging investors off-take partners and government stakeholders.

What makes Euroclean different from other refineries?

Three key advantages — powered by natural gas sourced below hub prices, near-zero sulfur diesel and jet fuel, and zero gas flaring meeting the highest global environmental standards.
